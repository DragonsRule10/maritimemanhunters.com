About us

We are the Maritime Manhunters from AMIkids Maritime Academy in Panama City, Florida. We are a team of high schoolers focused on STEM engagement and hands on learning through robotics. This is our first year doing First Tech Challenge but we are determined to make it one of the best! This year we are one of the only rookie teams that didn't use a kit and made ours with little to no directions. We are super excited to be competing this year and plan to keep competing in future years!
